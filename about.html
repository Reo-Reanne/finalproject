About McDonald's

McDonald's is a global fast food restaurant chain that serves millions of customers every day.

We pride ourselves on providing high-quality food, excellent customer service, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Our company history dates back to 1955 when Ray Kroc opened the first McDonald's restaurant in Des Plaines, Illinois.

At McDonald's, we strive to stand out from the competition by continuously innovating and introducing new menu items.
